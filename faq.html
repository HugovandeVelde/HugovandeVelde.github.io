Frequently Asked Questions

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

You can print a document from your laptop by visiting http://print.hz.nl After authenticating, you can upload the file you would like to print. Optionally you can choose whether you want to print in color or not. Select the HZ Printer or HZ Plotter to request the print job. Now you can Register using your HZ Pass on the TouchID next to one of the printers. Select "Print / Afdrukken" in the menu and your document will be printed

How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?

Register using your HZ Pass on the TouchID next to the printer. Select "Scanning - Scan" in the menu. Lay your sheet on the feeder or glass plate. Now press "Scan and Send", "Scan to me", "Yes" and hit the start button. Use the green button on the printer to scan page by page and press "Start Sending" when you\'re done.

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event at Friday 16/09) on the HZ web shop?

First go to https://webshop.hz.nl/ and log in with yout MyHz account. Add all items you want to the cart and when done, go to the shopping cart tab to checkout. Follow the instructions for your payment method and complete the payment.

Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID?

For all the latest news and updates regarding COVID you can visit the site https://hz.nl/en/current-affairs/coronavirus here you can also find what measures to take when you or a familiy member is infected

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

If you want to book a project space visit https://topdesk.hz.nl/tas/public/ssp/ Make sure you are logged in, and select "New Reservation" followed by "Book project group rooms" Now follow the instructions on the website and you're good to go

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Free parking is available at the car park at Poelendaelesingel 8 in Middelburg. This is at a few minutes walk from HZ. Students and staff can only enter by presenting their HZ pass at the barriers, guests can ring the bell at the barrier. After passing the barrier, follow the signs to the HZ parking places, marked with a white sign with the HZ logo.
